New Media Capstone

In Capstone, we were put into small groups to work on a term project that we would present at SLAM! My team members had diverse skills in design, marketing, and coding. We developed an app clip for a fictional comapny called Zoom Bikeshare to showcase the benefits of developing an app clip for businesses. An app clip is a lightweight app based on a fully developed app that does not take up storage on the user's phone and it is part of the latest iOS 14 features.
